Welcome to CLOS!

What is CLOS?

CLOS, also known as the Command Line Operating System, was originally a terminal program focused on mathematics that evolved into a user-friendly graphical application.

It's a comprehensive calculator featuring linear and quadratic equation solvers capable of handling complex numbers, along with advanced tools for graphing, calculus, trigonometry, logarithms, algebra, and much more.

The Story Behind CLOS

CLOS stands for "Command Line Operating System" and was originally conceived as a terminal-based operating system. However, realizing the limitations of a non-Unix-based OS without connectivity, the project evolved into a powerful mathematical tool that runs on existing systems.

What started as a command-line utility has now transformed into an intuitive graphical application, making advanced mathematics accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to helping students and educators by providing an accessible, offline mathematical tool that simplifies complex computations and creates stunning visualizations.

Our goal is to make advanced mathematical concepts approachable, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for the beauty of mathematics.
