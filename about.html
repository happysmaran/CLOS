Welcome to CLOS!

What is CLOS?

CLOS, also known as the Command Line Operating System, was originally a terminal program which is focused on Math that was later converted to a user-friendly GUI.

It is a calculator that comes with a linear and quadratic equation solver capable of handling non-real numbers.

It also has several features pertaining to graphing, derivatives, integrals, trigonometry, logarithms, algebra, and much more.

Why CLOS?

CLOS is a great tool for students and professionals alike. It is useful for students to learn and understand math concepts, and for professionals to quickly solve math problems.

It is also a great tool for teachers to quickly demonstrate certain math concepts to students, as it contains step-by-step solving.

Why the name "CLOS"?

CLOS stands for "Command Line Operating System," and was originally going to be a terminal-based operating system. But then I realized that a non-Unix-based OS with no connectivity is not a good idea.

Hence, I turned it into a terminal tool that runs on an existing system, and it is now a graphical application.
